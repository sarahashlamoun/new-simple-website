About Us

International Legal League is an organisation that aims at providing a diverse range of facilities to students across the globe, especially the ones who lack a support network.
The International Legal League is a community of law students and legal professionals from all over the world, aiming to collaborate and network beyond frontiers.

What do we do?

Through multiple means of communication (website, social media), our members help out and inform people on different areas of the law in different countries. With daily content such as articles, webinars, networking events and many more, our purpose is to spread awareness, reach out to discover further legal cultures and share our knowledge to enhance solidarity in the international legal community.

What can you do?

Always looking forward to welcoming new members and expanding our legal community across the globe, we recruit for the following roles:

  • Active team members, who would have a bigger implication in the organisation itself. Whether it is to manage a team, administrate, create events or deal with communication and marketing matters, this role requires a certain level of commitment.
  • Legal Ambassadors, who mainly represent their country by either advising on the law of their state or by writing content related to it. There could be more than one Ambassador for a country, however each country has a maximum of two Head Ambassadors. Being a Head Ambassador implies being responsible for the other ambassadors of the country represented, and appointing them.
  • Content Writers, to write frequently or occasionally on commercial awareness content, legal updates, or any other topic you think could be profitable and interesting for the international community.
  • Marketing and creators: We appreciate people who help us promote our organisation and help us understand the value of content creation. Memers are welcome!

However, if you are simply willing to meet law students or network with legal professionals from all over the world, then you can still join us and take part in our exciting range of activities.

If we convinced you, do join us in our journey to help law students around the world!

Explore the rest of our site to learn more about what we have to offer, and get in touch with any questions. International Legal League is here for you.
